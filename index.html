LATOS

This project work is aimed at investigating and comparing the strength of coarse aggregate and fine aggregate of different sources. These include both the physical and mechanical properties of the aggregate materials collected from crushed rubbles and sharp sand. The sample of the crushed rubbles is collected from a construction site in Surulere area of Lagos state
